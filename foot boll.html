FOOT-BALL

introdaction:-

"Football" ya "Soccer" ek prasiddh khel hai jo duniya bhar mein khela jaata hai. Yeh ek team sport hai jisme do teams, har ek mein 11 khiladi hote hain, ek football (gol) ko opposing team ke goal par maarne ki koshish karte hain. Yahaan kuch mukhya jaankariyan hain:

1. **Field**: Football ko khelne ke liye ek rectangular grassy field ka upyog hota hai. Field ka lambai lagbhag 100-110 meters aur chaudai 64-75 meters hoti hai.

2. **Goal**: Har ek field ke dono taraf ek gol hota hai. Gol ek horizontal crossbar aur vertical goalposts se bana hota hai.

3. **Ball**: Football ek spherical ball hoti hai jiska wajan lagbhag 400-450 grams hota hai aur diameter lagbhag 68-70 centimeters hota hai.

4. **Teams**: Football mein do teams hote hain, har ek mein 11 khiladi hote hain, jinmein se ek goalkeeper hota hai.

5. **Match Duration**: Ek football match lagbhag 90 minutes tak hoti hai, jisse do halves mein baanta jata hai, har ek ka samay 45 minutes hota hai. Halftime ke baad, teams ko sides change karne ka samay diya jata hai.

6. **Scoring**: Gol tab count hota hai jab ball opposing team ke goal ke andar jaata hai. Har gol ek point hoti hai.

7. **Fouls**: Football mein kuch niyam hote hain jo players ko karna ya nahi karna hota hai. Agar koi player niyamon ka ulanghan karta hai, toh referee fouls declare karta hai aur opposing team ko free kick ya penalty diya jaata hai.

8. **Tournaments**: Football ke kai pramukh tournaments hote hain, jaise FIFA World Cup, UEFA Champions League, Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, aur Bundesliga, jo duniya bhar mein lokpriya hai.

Yeh kuch mukhya jaankariyaan hain football ke khel ke baare mein. Yeh ek lokpriya aur prasiddh khel hai jo duniya bhar mein mahaan craze aur passion ke saath khela jaata hai.



rules:-

Football mein khel ke kuch basic rules hain:

1. **Goal**: Team ko opposition ke goal par ball ko daalna hai, goal ko score karne ke liye. Goal tab count hota hai jab ball poora goal line ke andar hota hai.

2. **Offside**: Ek player offside ho jata hai jab woh opposition ke last defender se closer hota hai jab ball unhein pass kiya jata hai. Offside tab count hota hai jab player ball ko receive karne ke time offside position mein hota hai.

3. **Foul**: Agar koi player dusre player ko haath lagata hai, unhein trip karta hai ya phir unke raste mein aakar unhein rokta hai, toh yeh foul hota hai.

4. **Penalty Kick**: Agar koi foul box ke andar hota hai, toh referee penalty kick award karta hai. Player ko ek chance milta hai goal ko score karne ka, jab woh aur goalkeeper ke beech mein ball ko rakh kar kick karta hai.

5. **Yellow Card/Red Card**: Agar koi player bahut bada foul karta hai ya fir uska behaviour achha nahi hota hai, toh referee unhein yellow card ya red card de sakta hai. Yellow card ek warning hoti hai aur red card ek player ko game se bahar kar deta hai.

6. **Throw-in**: Jab ball boundary line ke bahar jaata hai, toh game restart hota hai throw-in se. Team ke ek player ball ko dono haatho se pakad kar back ki taraf se boundary line ke pass se throw karta hai.

Yeh kuch basic football rules hain, aur game ke aur bhi rules hote hain jo game ke level ke hisaab se badal sakte hain.



itihaas:-

Football ka itihaas bohot purana hai aur yeh ek bahut prachin khel hai. Yeh game pehli baar kisi kisam ke formal rules ke saath England mein 19th century mein khela gaya tha.

Football ki modern version ka shuruwat England mein hua tha. 1863 mein, "Football Association" ne modern football ke rules ko sthapit kiya tha, jise ab "The Laws of the Game" kehte hain. Isse pehle, alag-alag prakar ke football khela jata tha, jiske rules har jagah alag the.

Pehle football match ka score tab kiya jata tha jab ek team doosri team se zyada goal kar leti thi. Lekin, 19th century ke dauran, goal posts lagane ki practice shuru hui aur goal posts ke beech mein ball ko pass karna compulsory hua. Isse hi goal concept taiyar hua.

Modern football ke rules aur structure gradually develop hue. 20th century mein, FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association) ne international football ke regulation ko manage karne ka kaam shuru kiya.

Aaj, football ek global khel hai jise hazaro countries khelte hain aur FIFA World Cup jaise bade tournaments duniya bhar mein excitement aur passion ka kendra banate hain.



facts:-

Football ek fascinating sport hai aur iske kai rochak facts hain:

1. **Sabse Purana Football Club**: Sheffield Football Club, jo England mein 1857 mein sthapit hua, duniya ka sabse purana football club hai.

2. **World Cup Trophy**: FIFA World Cup Trophy, jo duniya ke sabse prestigious football tournament ka symbol hai, pure gold mein bana hua hai aur uska weight lagbhag 6.1 kilograms hai.

3. **Maximum World Cup Wins**: Brazil ne sabse zyada FIFA World Cup jeete hain - 5 baar (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, aur 2002).

4. **Fastest Goal**: Fastest goal jo kabhi FIFA World Cup mein score kiya gaya tha, woh Hakan Şükür ke dwara tha jo Turkey ke taraf se 2002 mein 11 seconds mein kiya gaya tha.

5. **Largest Football Stadium**: Rungrado May Day Stadium, jo North Korea mein hai, duniya ka sabse bada football stadium hai. Iska seating capacity lagbhag 1,50,000 spectators hai.

6. **Professional Players**: FIFA estimates ke mutabiq, duniya bhar mein lagbhag 2.65 crore registered football players hain.

7. **Most Goals in a Single Match**: 2002 mein AS Adema aur SO l'Emyrne ke beech mein Madagascar Football Association Cup mein hui match mein AS Adema ne intentionally apne khiladiyon ko khelne se roka tha, iska parinaam yeh hua ke SO l'Emyrne ke khiladiyon ne apne hi goal par 149 goals khade kiye.

Yeh kuch rochak facts hain jo football ke baare mein hain. Ye sirf trailer hai, is game ke aur bhi kai rochak aur amazing facts hain.
