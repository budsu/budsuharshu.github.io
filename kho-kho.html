KHO-KHO

introdaction:-

**Kho-Kho** ek pramukh pratiyogita wala aur lokpriya outdoor khel hai jo bharat mein khele jata hai. Yeh ek fast-paced game hai, jismein do teams hote hain - ek attacking team aur ek defending team.

**Khel ki field** ek rectangle shape ki hoti hai, jise 'court' kehte hain. Court mein boundary lines hoti hain aur ek khel ke dauraan player ko in boundaries ke andar hi rehna hota hai. Ismein ek central lane hoti hai jo 'Kho-Kho' line kehte hain, aur dusri team players ko tag karne ke liye yeh lane ka istemal hota hai.

**Khel ki prakriya**:

1. **Teams ki positions**: Har team mein 12 players hote hain. Har team ek dusre team ko pehle chase karne ke liye taiyar hoti hai (attacking team), jabki doosri team (defending team) players ko tag karne aur apne player ko bachane ke liye taiyar hoti hai.

2. **Gameplay ki shuruaat**: Game ki shuruaat mein, attacking team ke ek player ko field mein bheja jata hai aur unhein 9 seconds diye jate hain dusre team ke players ko tag karne ke liye. Unhein 'Chaser' kehte hain. Jab yeh player kisi defending team ke player ko tag karta hai, toh woh player tag ho jata hai aur apni team ke liye out ho jata hai.

3. **Defending team ki baari**: Jab ek player ko tag kiya jata hai, toh woh player field ke bahar jaata hai aur defending team ke ek naya player aata hai, jo apne team ke liye field ke andar jaata hai. Yeh unka 'turn' hota hai.

4. **Kho-Kho line**: Players ko Kho-Kho line ko touch karke wapas ana hota hai jab unki bari hoti hai, yani jab unhein tag karne bheja jata hai.

5. **Strategy**: Har team ko ek behtar strategy banana hota hai apne players ko defend karne ke liye aur attacking team ke players ko tag karne ke liye.

6. **Winning**: Game ka objective hai opposing team ke sabhi players ko tag karke unhein out kar dena. Kho-Kho ek teamwork aur physical fitness ko badhane wala game hai. Yeh game bharat mein kai generations se khela ja raha hai aur ab bhi kai schools, colleges aur tournaments mein popular hai.



rules:-



Kho-Kho ek prasiddh aur lokpriya bharatiya khel hai jo samaj mein vikas aur sangathan ko badhava dene ke liye prashikshit hota hai. Yeh ek timahi khel hai jismein do teams hote hain. Neeche diye gaye hain Kho-Kho ke kuch pramukh niyam:

1. **Teams**: Har team mein 12 khiladi hote hain, jinme se 9 field khiladi hote hain aur 3 reserve khiladi hote hain.

2. **Playing Area**: Kho-Kho khel ke maidan ki lambai 29 meter aur chaudai 16 meter hoti hai. Maidan ko 2 equal parts mein divide kiya jata hai, jismein ek team chhupa hota hai aur doosra team kuch cheezon se bani line ke peeche hoti hai.

3. **Game Duration**: Kho-Kho ki ek daud mein samay simit hota hai. Generally, ek daud mein 9 minutes ka samay hota hai.

4. **Tagging**: Ek team ke khiladi, jo 'chhupo' kshetra mein hote hain, doosre team ke khiladi ko 'tag' karne ki koshish karte hain. Jab koi khiladi tag hota hai, woh maidan ke beech mein aa jata hai.

5. **Defensive Strategy**: Chhupne wale khiladiyon ko apne peeche se tag karne ke liye khiladiyon ko kuch rules ka palan karna padta hai. Woh sabhi khiladiyon ko ek hi disha mein daudne ki permission nahi dete hain.

6. **Turns**: Har ek team ka ek hi din hota hai chhupa hua team banne ka aur doosre din se khelne ka. Ye rotaational hota hai.

7. **Out**: Jab koi khiladi tag ho jata hai ya phir woh time se pehle apne pairon ke oopar nahi rehta, toh use out mana jata hai. Woh us daud ke baad dubara maidan mein aata hai.

8. **Winner**: Team jo sabse kam samay mein sabhi khiladiyon ko tag karti hai, ya maximum khiladiyon ko tag karke jit jati hai, woh vijeta hoti hai. Yeh kuch mukhya niyam hain jo Kho-Kho khel mein aam taur par laagu kiye jate hain. Niyam sthaniy star par alag-alag sansthaon ke antargat bhi alag ho sakte hain.



indian kho-kho players:-

Bharat mein kho-kho bhi ek lokapriya khel hai aur desh ke kai pratibhagat kho-kho khiladi hain, jo antarrashtriya star par bhi pradarshan karte hain. Yahan kuch pramukh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladiyon ke naam hain:

1. **Neha Rathi**: Neha Rathi ek pramukh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jinho ne desh ke liye anek antarrashtriya star par pradarshan kiya hai. Unka tej gati aur nipunata ne unhe mukhya sthan dilaya hai.

2. **Savita Punia**: Savita Punia bhi ek mahan Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jinhone antarrashtriya star par bhi pradarshan kiya hai. Unka yogdan Bharatiya kho-kho ke vikas mein mahatvapurn hai.

3. **Manpreet Kaur**: Manpreet Kaur ek anya prasiddh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jinhone desh ke liye samman janak pradarshan kiya hai. Unka yogdan Bharatiya kho-kho mein mahilaon ke uchch star tak pahunchne mein mahatvapurn hai.

4. **Raju Dabhade**: Raju Dabhade ek prasiddh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jo apne samriddh pradarshan aur kaptani ke liye mashhoor hain. Unka yogdan Bharatiya kho-kho mein mukhya sthan dilane mein mahatvapurn hai.

5. **Rakesh Kumar**: Rakesh Kumar bhi ek pramukh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jinhone desh ke liye anek samman janak pradarshan kiya hai. Unka samarpan aur yogdan Bharatiya kho-kho ko badhava dene mein mahatvapurn hai.

Ye kuch pramukh Bharatiya kho-kho khiladi hain, jo desh ke liye pratibaddh hain aur antarrashtriya star par bhi pradarshan karte hain.



facts:-

Kho-Kho ek prachin Bharatiya khel hai, jo ki samajik aur sharirik roop se mazbooti aur samriddhi ko badhane ke liye prachlit kiya jata hai. Yeh hai Kho-Kho ki kuch mahatvapurn ghatnayein:

1. **Prarambh**: Kho-Kho ka janm Bharat mein hua tha, aur iska ullekh itihaasik granth Ramayana mein bhi milta hai. Yeh khel mahilao aur purusho dono ke liye popular hai.

2. **19th Century**: Kho-Kho ka prachar aur vikas 19th century mein hua, jab British India mein schools aur colleges mein isko ek pramukh khel ke roop mein shamil kiya gaya. Isse samaj mein swasthya aur samajik ekta ki bhavna ko badhaya gaya.

3. **Kho-Kho Association**: Kho-Kho ke vikas ke liye Bharat Kho-Kho Association (AKKA) ka gathan 1955 mein hua. Iska uddeshya Kho-Kho ko desh bhar mein pracharit karna aur ismein vikas karna hai.

4. **International Recognition**: Kho-Kho ko 1982 mein pehli baar South Asian Games mein shamil kiya gaya tha. Iske baad se iska craze aur popularity tezi se badh gaya aur aaj yeh ek antarrashtriya star ka khel hai.

5. **World Kho-Kho Championship**: World Kho-Kho Championship pehli baar 1996 mein aayojit kiya gaya tha. Ismein desh bhar se Kho-Kho ke shuruaati khiladiyon ne bhag liya aur isne Kho-Kho ko antarrashtriya manch par pahunchaya.

6. **Asian Kho-Kho Championship**: Asian Kho-Kho Championship bhi regular roop se aayojit hota hai, jismein Asian countries ke kho-kho teams bhag lete hain aur ek doosre se bhidte hain.

7. **Modernization**: Aaj ke samay mein, Kho-Kho ka mahatva kam ho gaya hai kuch urban areas mein, lekin kai jagahon par iska prachar aur vikas hota ja raha hai. Schools, colleges, aur communities mein Kho-Kho ke prati interest badhane ke liye various initiatives liye ja rahe hain.

Yeh kuch mahatvapurn ghatnayein hain jo Kho-Kho ki itihaas aur vikas ko darshati hain. Yah khel aaj bhi desh bhar mein khela jata hai aur uska mahatva swasthya aur samajik ekta mein hai.
