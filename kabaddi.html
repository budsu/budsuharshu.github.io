KABADDI

introdaction:-

yeh rahi kabaddi ke baare mein kuch mahatvapurn jaankari:
**Kabaddi**:
- **Vyaakhya**: Kabaddi ek prachin Bharatiya mool ke khel hai jo pramukh roop se deshon jaise Bharat, Pakistan, Bangladesh, aur Iran mein prachalit hai. Is khel mein do teams hote hain, jo ek doosre ke khilaadiyon ko tag karne ki koshish karte hain, ek saath mein saans lete hue, apne taraf se shuru hokar khiladiyon ko apne prativarshiyon tak le jaane ki koshish karte hain.
- **Prakar**:

1. **Ghar ke Kabaddi**: Is prakar mein, khilaadiyon ko ek hi taraf mein khada hona hota hai aur ek vyakti baar-baar doosre team ke khilaadiyon ko tag karne ke liye jaata hai.

2. **Pro Kabaddi League (PKL)**: Yeh Bharat ki sabse pramukh kabaddi league hai, jismein desh aur videshi khiladiyon ki teams shaamil hoti hain aur mahilaon ki bhi league hoti hai.
- **Itihas**: Kabaddi ka itihas prachin hai aur iska ullekh Mahabharat mein bhi milta hai. Is khel ko kai prakar se alag-alag prantoon mein khela jaata hai, jaise ki Sanjha Punjab (Punjab region) mein, Hadudu Gujarat (Gujarat region) mein, aur Chedugudu Andhra Pradesh aur Telangana (Andhra Pradesh and Telangana regions) mein.
- **Sanskaran**: Kabaddi khelne ke liye khaas sanskaran ki zaroorat hoti hai, jaise ki:
- **Kabaddi Ki Dhaal**: Yeh khiladiyon ko dusre team ke khilaadiyon ko tag karne ke liye istemal hoti hai.
- **Khel Kshetra**: Kabaddi ke khel kshetra ka aakar aur dhancha niyamit hota hai, jisse khel ki maanak lambaai aur chaudaai tai hoti hai.
- **Kapde**: Khiladiyon ko samanya roop se khaas uniform pehnne ki aavashyakta hoti hai.
- **Pratiyogitaayen**: Kabaddi ki pratiyogitaayen lagbhag har varsh ayojit hoti hain, jismein antarraashtreeya star par 'Kabaddi World Cup' shaamil hai.
- **Prasiddhi**: Kabaddi Bharat mein ek lokpriya khel hai aur Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) ke aane se iska prasiddhi aur uchch star ka pratyaksh karyakram ban gaya hai.
- **Kushaltaayen**: Kabaddi mein sharirik sanchaar, daurna, aur dimaag ka tej chalana mahatvapurna hai. Khilaadiyon ko apni shaktiyon ka pura istemal karke aur sahi samay par samne ke khilaadiyon ko tag karke points prapt karna hota hai.

Yeh kuch mahatvapurn jaankariyan hain. Agar aapko kabaddi ke baare mein aur jaankari chahiye, toh pooch sakte hain!



rules:-

kabaddi ke niyam yeh hote hain:

1. **Teams**: Kabaddi mein do teams hote hain, har ek team mein 7 khiladi hote hain.

2. **Khel Kshetra**: Kabaddi ka khel kshetra ek rectangular maidan hota hai, jisey dimaag ke samlaingik ho.

3. **Start**: Khel ek umpire ke shuruwat dene par hota hai. Har team ke liye ek-ek khiladi keval apne teritory se bahar aake shuruwat karta hai.

4. **Challenger**: Shuruaati khiladi (raider) doosri team ke khiladiyon ko tag karne ke liye unke taraf jaata hai. Usko saans lene ki awashyakta hai aur 'kabaddi, kabaddi' bolna padta hai, bina kisi chetawani ke.

5. **Tagging**: Challenger ko doosri team ke khiladiyon ko tag karke vapas apne teritory tak jaana hota hai. Agar wo tag hote hue wapas apne teritory tak nahi pahunchta, toh wo 'out' hota hai.

6. **Defending Team**: Jab ek khiladi ko tag kiya jata hai, tab uski team uske prati koi pratikriya karne ka pramaan nahi kar sakti. Uska kewal ek hi rasta hota hai, woh bhaag jaye ya tag kare.

7. **Raider ko Defence karna**: Jab ek khiladi shuruaati khiladi (raider) ke taraf jaata hai, toh uske team ke khiladiyon ka kaam hota hai use tag karne ka, taki wo apne teritory wapas na jaye.

8. **Points**: Points tab milte hain jab ek raider apne teritory wapas pahunchta hai bina tag huye, ya agar usne doosri team ke khiladiyon ko tag kar diya hai.

9. **Out**: Ek khiladi 'out' ho jata hai agar usne kabaddi ke niyamon ka ullanghan kiya ho, jaise agar raider ko tag kiya gaya ho, ya agar raider apne teritory wapas nahi jaata.

10. **Winning**: Team jo sabse zyada points score karti hai, wo jeet jaati hai.

Yeh kuch mool niyam hain kabaddi ke. Khel ki aur bhi niyamon ki jaankari aapko niyamak sanchalakon se ya sammanit sroton se mil sakti hai.



itihaas:-

Kabaddi ek prachin khel hai jiska itihaas Bharat se juda hua hai. Iska ullekh Bharat ke prachin granthon mein milta hai aur iske anek prakar aur roop aaye aur gaye hain.

Kabaddi ke prarambhik roop ko vishesh roop se deshon jaise Bharat, Pakistan, Bangladesh, aur Iran mein khela jaata hai. Yeh khel gaon-gaon mein, khaaskar kisanon aur yuvakon ke beech mein, lokpriya tha. Kabaddi ki pramukh visheshta yeh hai ki ismein koi bhi vishesh saman ki avashyakta nahi hoti, sirf ek khiladi ki himmat aur daurne ki shamta ka praman hota hai.

Kabaddi ke prati jan-jan ki bhavukata aur prabhav ne is khel ko anek roopon mein badal diya. Vibhinn pranton mein, jaise ki Punjab, Gujarat, aur Andhra Pradesh, alag-alag roopon mein kabaddi khela jaata hai, jismein niyamon aur prakriyaon mein chhote-chhote antar hote hain.

Is prachin khel ne bhi aaj kal aadhunikata ko sparsh kiya hai. Pro Kabaddi League (PKL), jisey 2014 mein shuru kiya gaya tha, Bharat mein kabaddi ko naye unchaayiyon tak pahunchaya hai. PKL mein desh aur videsh ke pramukh khiladiyon ki teams shaamil hoti hain aur yeh ek mahine tak chalne wala mahotsav hota hai.

Kabaddi ka itihaas Bharatiya sanskriti aur virasat ka ek mahatvapurna hissa hai, jiska jeevan aur prabhav aaj bhi dikhayi deta hai. Is khel ki prachin virasat ko aaj bhi sammaan diya jata hai aur uska mahatva desh aur videsh mein badh raha hai.
