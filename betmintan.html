BETMINTAN

introdaction:-

Badminton ek prasiddh indoor khel hai, jismein do ya char khiladi ek chhota sa shuttlecock ka upyog karte hain jo ek net ke dono taraf se hota hai. Yeh khel sharirik pratibadhata, tez soch aur chusti laata hai. Yahan kuch mukhya jaankariyan hain:

1. **Court**: Badminton court ek rectangular shape ka hota hai, jismein ek net hota hai jo court ko do hisson mein baantta hai. Singles khelne ke liye court ki lambai 13.4 meters aur chaudai 5.18 meters hoti hai, jabki doubles ke liye lambai 13.4 meters aur chaudai 6.1 meters hoti hai.

2. **Shuttlecock**: Shuttlecock ek chhota sa gol ya conical shape ka hota hai, jo plastic ya natural feathers se bana hota hai. Ye shuttlecock khiladiyon ke racket se maar kar dusre taraf ki team ke khiladi ke taraf bheja jaata hai.

3. **Rackets**: Har khiladi ke paas ek racket hoti hai, jise wo shuttlecock ko maarne ke liye istemal karta hai. Rackets ki handle lambi hoti hai aur unki body mei halka aur durable material ka upyog hota hai.

4. **Service**: Game shuru karne ke liye ek khiladi apne side se shuttlecock ko maar kar dusre side ki team ke taraf bhejta hai. Service tab tak hoti hai jab tak ek khiladi point na jeet le ya na hi doosra team point na jeet le.

5. **Scoring System**: Badminton mein rally ke bharose point system hota hai, yani har ek rally ke baad point milta hai. Match ke pratham 21 points tak hoti hai, aur jis team ko pehle 21 points jeet leti hai woh match jeet jaati hai, lekin agar score 20-20 tak barabar hota hai to ek khiladi ko do points ka farak hona zaroori hota hai tak match jeet sake.

6. **Olympics**: Badminton ko Olympics mein 1992 se shamil kiya gaya tha, aur isse pehle yeh sirf ek demonstration sport tha.

Yeh kuch mukhya jaankariyaan hain jo badminton ke khel ke baare mein hain. Yeh ek lokpriya aur prasiddh khel hai jo duniya bhar mein khela jaata hai.



rules:-

Badminton ke khel ke niyam nimn hai:

1. **Service**: Service ke liye, ek khiladi ko shuttlecock ko apne side ke piche se, net ke upar maarna hota hai, dusre taraf ki team ke khiladi ke taraf. Shuttlecock ko ek baar maarne ke baad, woh opposing team ke khiladi ke taraf bheji jaati hai.

2. **Rally**: Jab shuttlecock ek baar service karne ke baad court ke andar hota hai, toh khiladiyon ke beech mein rally shuru hoti hai. Khiladiyon ko shuttlecock ko maar kar opposing team ke taraf bhejna hota hai, bina ki court ke boundary ko chhune.

3. **Point Jeetna**: Point tab jeeta jaata hai jab opposing team ke khiladi shuttlecock ko haar jata hai ya agar wo shuttlecock ko court ke boundary mein maarne mein asafal rehta hai.

4. **Scoring System**: Badminton mein rally ke bharose point system hota hai. Har rally ke baad ek point milta hai. Match usually 21 points tak hoti hai.

5. **Service Rotation**: Jab ek team ek point jeet leti hai, toh service rotate hoti hai aur wo team jo point jeeti hai woh next rally mein service karta hai.

6. **Faults**: Kuch common faults badminton mein ye hote hain:
- Shuttlecock ko court ke boundary mein nahi maar paana.
- Net ko touch karna shuttlecock ko maarne ki koshish karte waqt.

- Service line ko cross nahi karna. - Shuttlecock ko ek hi baar maar kar dobara nahi maarna.

Yeh kuch mukhya niyam hote hain badminton ke khel ke. Iske alawa tournament ya match ke specific rules bhi hote hain jo ki organizers ke dwara tai kiye jaate hain.



itihaas:-

Badminton ka itihaas kafi purana hai aur yeh prasiddh outdoor khel, "Poona" ya "Battledore and Shuttlecock," se prarambh hua. Iska shuruaati roop 19th century ke England mein tha, jahan isse "Badminton" ke naam se jaana jaata tha. Badminton shabd ka upyog "Badminton House" naamak ek sthal se liya gaya tha, jahan is khel ki shuruat hui thi.

1860s mein, British Indian Army ke faujis ne is khel ko India mein popular kiya aur us samay se yeh khel Bharat mein bhi prachalit ho gaya. 1873 mein, Duke of Beaufort ne Gloucestershire ke Badminton House mein ek badminton party aayojit ki thi, jismein unhone is khel ka formalized version khela. Yeh party badminton ka mahatvapurn itihaasik sthal maani jaati hai.

Badminton ki pratham World Championship 1977 mein London mein aayojit ki gayi thi. Iske baad se yeh khel din pratidin lokpriya hota gaya aur aaj duniya bhar mein khela jaata hai. Badminton ko 1992 se Olympics mein shamil kiya gaya tha, aur isse pehle yeh sirf ek demonstration sport tha.

Badminton ka itihaas aur uska vikas pramukh aayojano, khiladiyon aur prashikshakon ki mehnat aur sahyog ke madhyam se hota raha hai.
